Shanivarwada

Gandhi National Memorial Society’s Agakhan Palace is the Italian arches and lawns. This place has been used as a prison by Britishers for Mahatma Gandhi, Kasturba Gandhi and Mahadeobhai Desai during the ‘Quit India Movement’.

Marble memorials were put later on in the memory of Kasturba and Mahadeobhai. as both of them breathed their last in this place.
